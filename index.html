Community rules.

Three commitments that keep this community valuable for everyone.

Engage consistently

Show up to discussions, podcasts, and challenges. Members who stay active unlock mentorship, referrals, and placement support.

No spam or self-promotion Strict

Irrelevant messages, course promotions, or unsolicited outreach result in an immediate and permanent ban from the community.

Respect privacy Enforced

Direct messages to members or mentors require explicit admin consent. This protects everyone's time and keeps the space professional.
